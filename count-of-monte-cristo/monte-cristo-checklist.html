How to Use This Ledger
  1. Choose the day you're starting the book and the day you'd like to finish it, then click Fix the Terms.
  2. Already partway through? Enter the chapter you're on and earlier chapters are marked read automatically, listed below with an unknown completion date.
  3. The ledger splits the book's 117 chapters evenly across your remaining days and shows today's quota. Once today's quota is checked off, today's card disappears — it won't refill until tomorrow.
  4. Check off chapters as you read them — your progress is saved automatically in this browser.
  5. Miss a day? The ledger reshuffles what's left across the days that remain.
  6. Changed your mind about the deadline? Click Renegotiate the terms of release to pick new dates.
